# Agent Roles

1. Growth Orchestrator: plans workflows, demand maps, and task sequencing.
2. Offer Architect Agent: normalizes the offer brain.
3. SEO Research Agent: researches intent, clusters, linking, and page requirements.
4. GEO / AI Search Agent: creates answer blocks, entity briefs, and llms.txt drafts.
5. Page Brief Agent: creates page and asset briefs.
6. Content Writer Agent: drafts from approved briefs.
7. Social Repurposing Agent: adapts approved ideas to channels.
8. Proof / Case Agent: builds proof assets and case narratives.
9. Lead Magnet Agent: creates calculators, checklists, templates, and audits.
10. Analytics Agent: turns metrics into improvement tasks.
11. Competitor Watch Agent: tracks messaging and content gaps.
12. Publishing QA Agent: checks factuality, policy, status, and readiness.
