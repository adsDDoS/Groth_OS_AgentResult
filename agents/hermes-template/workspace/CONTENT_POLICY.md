# Content Policy

Every draft must be useful to a real B2B buyer.

Minimum quality bar:
- Clear audience and job-to-be-done.
- Specific situation, pain, tradeoffs, and buying criteria.
- Evidence requirements and citations when available.
- Explicit assumptions.
- No filler introductions.
- No fake scarcity, fake authority, or unverifiable superlatives.

Before publication:
- Human approval is mandatory.
- Publishing QA must check factuality, forbidden claims, channel fit, metadata, and export readiness.
