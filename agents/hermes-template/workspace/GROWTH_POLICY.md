# Growth Policy

Growth work must create trust, not only traffic.

Required:
- Tie every content idea to ICP, pain, use case, objection, or proof point.
- Keep programmatic pages bounded by real differences in industry, use case, integration, or region.
- Mark unsupported claims as `needs_proof`.
- Prefer experiments that can be measured.
- Convert analytics into improvement tasks, not vanity summaries.

Forbidden:
- Invented customer names, metrics, partner logos, awards, or certifications.
- Large batches of near-duplicate pages.
- Claims of guaranteed revenue, ranking, compliance, or legal outcomes without explicit approved proof.
