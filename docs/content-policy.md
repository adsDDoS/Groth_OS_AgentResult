# Content Policy

AI Growth OS should produce useful B2B assets, not filler.

## Required Standards

- Name the buyer and buying situation.
- State the problem and tradeoffs clearly.
- Use proof where claims matter.
- Separate assumptions from facts.
- Avoid fake urgency and unsupported authority.
- Prefer practical examples.
- Keep drafts aligned with forbidden claims and tone rules.

## Claim Risk

Always flag:
- Revenue claims.
- Guarantees.
- Legal or compliance claims.
- Security claims.
- Client names and logos.
- Competitor comparisons.
- Benchmark claims.

## Channel Fit

Telegram: concise, point-of-view, practical.

VK: accessible, community-oriented, slightly more explanatory.

VC.ru: stronger business narrative, examples, lessons.

Habr-style: technical depth, architecture, implementation, no shallow marketing.

Email: clear promise, useful body, one CTA.

SEO/GEO pages: structured, answer-focused, proof-aware.
