# Product

AI Growth OS is a B2B growth operating system for companies that need systematic demand creation, not random AI content.

The product helps teams:
- Describe their offer and proof.
- Build a demand map across products, pains, industries, comparisons, integrations, cases, FAQs, tools, and regions.
- Create SEO and GEO-ready briefs and pages.
- Repurpose approved ideas into Telegram, VK, VC.ru, Habr-style, email, partner, and manual export assets.
- Build proof assets, case studies, calculators, checklists, audit forms, and lead magnets.
- Track competitors and content gaps.
- Turn analytics into improvement tasks.
- Control all sensitive publishing through approvals.

## Positioning

AI Growth OS is not an AI writer. It is a backend-first operating layer for B2B growth operations.

The source of truth is Postgres. Agents are workers. The backend controls tenants, tasks, approvals, publishing, audit, and integrations.

## Manual-First Principle

Every customer can start without channel APIs.

The system can produce an approved content pack for a week or campaign, including SEO pages, social posts, articles, email, lead magnets, and a publishing calendar CSV.

API publishing can be added later per channel.
