# SEO/GEO Playbook

AI Growth OS treats SEO and GEO as evidence architecture, not keyword volume.

## Good Page Types

- Product pages.
- Pain pages.
- Industry pages with real industry-specific detail.
- Comparison pages with sourced claims.
- Integration pages with actual integration workflows.
- Glossary pages tied to buyer education.
- Calculator/tool pages.
- Case pages.
- FAQ pages.
- Alternative pages.

## Programmatic SEO Rules

Allowed:
- Distinct pages based on real differences.
- Region pages with unique local constraints, proof, language, regulation, or buyer behavior.
- Industry pages with real workflows and buying criteria.

Rejected:
- 1000 near-identical region pages.
- Keyword-swapped pages.
- Unsupported rankings or guarantees.
- Fake local proof.

## GEO / AI Search

Create:
- Direct answer blocks.
- Entity definitions.
- FAQ blocks.
- Source pages for proof.
- llms.txt drafts.
- Schema recommendations.

Each answer block should be concise, attributable, and safe to quote.
